Origins

The website's original purpose was to help friends bridge the gap from having 10 players to getting in game with fair teams so that everybody could have a fun time playing League of Legends.
You might be wondering what "LRR" is supposed to mean. LRR stands for "League Round Robin" because one night some good friends managed to gather 20 people together to form 4 teams to play a series of games in a round-robin fashion. However ironic is the fact that this website does not (yet) have any functionality to draft multiple teams for round-robin systems, the name has stuck. Every visual element on the modern art at the bottom of the page a memorial to days past.
Come, we need 1 more for 5v5s. For the old times.
